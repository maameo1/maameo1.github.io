About

I'm a first-year PhD student at the University of Lincoln, supervised by Dr. James Brown, Dr. Wenting Duan, and Dr. Moazzam Jawaid. My research focuses on making deep learning work with limited labelled data for coronary artery segmentation and acute coronary syndrome prediction.

Previously, I conducted neuroimaging research at Washington University in St. Louis, studying brain structure in movement disorders. I also built research tools — including ResearchHub, an AI-powered literature review platform.
